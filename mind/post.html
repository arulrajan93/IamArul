Using AI as a thinking partner, not a shortcut

December 2025 · Systems · AI

Most people approach AI with one question: How fast can I get the answer?

I approach it differently. I ask: How can this help me think better?

Speed vs clarity

Speed amplifies direction. If the direction is wrong, speed only makes things worse.

That’s why I use AI to:

  • Challenge my assumptions
  • Reframe problems
  • Force clearer thinking

The output is secondary. The thinking is the work.
